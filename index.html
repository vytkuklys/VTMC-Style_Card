Ant

An ant is a symbol of strength, diligence, honesty, unity, patience, insignificance, stamina, sacrifice and loyalty.

  • Scientific name: Formicidae.
  • Number of species: over 12,000.
  • Biomass: approx. equal to 800mm people.
  • Lifespan: 6 weeks - 25 years.

Ants are insects that live in colonies. They are useful since they dig tunnels of labyrinths for more water to reach soil and roots of plants.
